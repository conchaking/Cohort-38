Bio

Adam was born in the wonderful location of Indianapolis, Indiana where he grew up until the age of 22. He enjoyed playing video games, sports (mainly basketball), leanring new skills, and eventually went away for college in 2006.


After his freshmen year of college, Adam realized that the military would pay for his school, and entered the service (Navy) one year after his freshmen year of college. He eventually receieved his first assignment, and the long journey ahead of him would began.


He was stationed in Virginia Beach, VA for nearly 13 months before finally traveling to his first command in Naples, Itlay aka Napoli where he discovered his love for seafood, margherita pizza, Europe in general, and soccer futbol.


Adam left his wonderful experiences of Itlay and brought those memories to San Diego, CA where he found love, his first wife, first hand the beauty that Southern California has to offer. He lived in cities such as Ocean Beach, El Cajon, before making Chula Vista his home.
